Frequently Asked Questions

Account Management

Click the "Register" button in the top right corner and fill out the registration form. You'll need to provide:
  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Password
  • Role (Buyer or Seller)

Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email.

Buying

Browse items, click on one you're interested in, and use the "Add to Cart" button. Follow the checkout process to complete your purchase.

We accept credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. All transactions are secure and encrypted.

Selling

Log in as a seller, go to your dashboard, and click "Add New Item". Fill in the item details, upload photos, and set your price.

We charge a small commission of 5% on successful sales. There are no listing fees.

Security

We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.

Contact our support team immediately through the Contact page. We take security concerns seriously.
