Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

At Thrash and Treasure Platform, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your data.

1. Data Collection

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, date of birth
  • Account Information: Login credentials, user preferences
  • Transaction Data: Purchase history, payment details
  • Usage Data: Browsing history, device information
  • Communication Data: Messages, reviews, feedback

2. Data Usage

We use your data to:

  • Process transactions and payments
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve our services
  • Send relevant notifications
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security

3. Data Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Payment processors for transaction handling
  • Delivery partners for order fulfillment
  • Legal authorities when required by law
  • Service providers who assist our operations

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored:

  • On secure servers with encryption
  • In accordance with data protection laws
  • For the duration necessary to fulfill our services
  • With regular backups and security updates

5. Security Measures

We protect your data through:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Employee training on data protection

6. Cookies Policy

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze site traffic and usage
  • Provide personalized content
  • Improve site functionality

7. User Rights

You have the right to:

8. Children's Privacy
